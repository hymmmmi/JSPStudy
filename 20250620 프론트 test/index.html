BALLET

Ballet is an art genre that requires a dancer's sense of balance, flexibility, strength, and expressiveness. Ballet is a form of performing art created by harmoniously harmonizing music and dance. This art is accomplished through elegant and elaborate movements and conveys the stories and emotions drawn by dancing performers.

CONTEMPORARY DANCE

Contemporary dance is a style of expressive movement that combines elements of several dance genres including modern, jazz, lyrical, and ballet. It emphasizes versatility, improvisation, and freedom of movement. Dancers often use floor work, fall and recovery, and fluid transitions. It is known for conveying emotions and telling stories through abstract and creative choreography.

KIDS BALLET

Children's ballet is a beginner-friendly dance class designed for young kids. It teaches basic ballet steps, posture, and rhythm in a fun and gentle way. Classes help improve coordination, flexibility, and confidence. It also encourages creativity and a love for dance through music and movement.
